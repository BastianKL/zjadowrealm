Simple Chicken & Rice

Prep: 10 min | Cook: 25 min | Serves: 2
Ingredients:
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 cup white rice
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
Instructions:
  1. Season chicken with salt and pepper
  2. Heat oil in pan, cook chicken 6-7 min each side
  3. Remove chicken, sauté onion and garlic
  4. Add rice, stir 1 minute
  5. Add broth, bring to boil
  6. Return chicken, cover, simmer 18 minutes
  7. Let rest 5 minutes, then serve

Pasta with Vegetables

Prep: 15 min | Cook: 20 min | Serves: 2
Ingredients:
  • 300g pasta (any shape)
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 200g cherry tomatoes
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • Fresh basil, salt, pepper
Instructions:
  1. Cook pasta according to package directions
  2. Heat oil in large pan
  3. Sauté bell pepper and zucchini 5 minutes
  4. Add tomatoes and garlic, cook 3 minutes
  5. Season with salt and pepper
  6. Drain pasta, toss with vegetables
  7. Garnish with fresh basil and serve

Easy Baked Salmon

Prep: 5 min | Cook: 15 min | Serves: 2
Ingredients:
  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried herbs
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Optional: side vegetables
Instructions:
  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F)
  2. Place salmon on baking sheet
  3. Brush with olive oil and garlic
  4. Season with herbs, salt, and pepper
  5. Top with lemon slices
  6. Bake for 12-15 minutes
  7. Serve with your favorite vegetables

Fluffy Scrambled Eggs

Prep: 2 min | Cook: 5 min | Serves: 1
Ingredients:
  • 3-4 large eggs
  • 2 tbsp milk or cream
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Optional: cheese, herbs
  • Toast for serving
Instructions:
  1. Crack eggs into bowl, add milk
  2. Whisk well, season with salt and pepper
  3. Melt butter in non-stick pan over low heat
  4. Pour in eggs, don't stir immediately
  5. Gently stir with rubber spatula every 30 seconds
  6. Remove from heat while slightly wet
  7. Serve immediately with toast

Hearty Vegetable Soup

Prep: 15 min | Cook: 30 min | Serves: 4
Ingredients:
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 potatoes, cubed
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, herbs
Instructions:
  1. Heat oil in large pot
  2. Sauté onion until soft, 5 minutes
  3. Add carrots and potatoes
  4. Pour in broth and tomatoes
  5. Bring to boil, then simmer 20 minutes
  6. Season to taste
  7. Serve hot with crusty bread

Quick Ground Beef Stir-fry

Prep: 10 min | Cook: 15 min | Serves: 3
Ingredients:
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 bell peppers, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt, pepper to taste
Instructions:
  1. Heat oil in large pan or wok
  2. Add ground beef, cook until browned
  3. Add onions, cook 3 minutes
  4. Add peppers and garlic
  5. Stir-fry for 5-7 minutes
  6. Add soy sauce, season to taste
  7. Serve with rice or noodles

Cooking Tips

Time Saving
  • Prep vegetables when you have time
  • Cook larger batches for leftovers
  • Use frozen vegetables when fresh aren't available
Shopping
  • Buy basic ingredients in bulk
  • Keep pantry staples stocked
  • Plan meals around seasonal produce
Cooking
  • Taste and adjust seasoning
  • Don't overcook vegetables
  • Let meat rest after cooking
