Sample Meal Plan
Breakfast
  • Greek yogurt with berries and nuts
  • Oatmeal with banana and peanut butter
  • Eggs with spinach and whole grain toast
Lunch
  • Quinoa bowl with vegetables and chicken
  • Salmon salad with mixed greens
  • Lentil soup with whole grain bread
Dinner
  • Grilled fish with roasted vegetables
  • Lean beef with sweet potato
  • Tofu stir-fry with brown rice
Snacks
  • Apple with almond butter
  • Greek yogurt with honey
  • Mixed nuts and dried fruit
Hydration & Timing
Daily Hydration Goals
  • 8-10 glasses of water daily
  • Extra 16-20oz per hour of exercise
  • Monitor urine color (light yellow = good)
  • Start each day with a glass of water
Meal Timing Tips
  • Eat within 2 hours of waking
  • 3 main meals + 1-2 healthy snacks
  • Stop eating 2-3 hours before bed
  • Pre/post workout nutrition matters
Foods to Limit
  • Processed and packaged foods
  • Sugary drinks and desserts
  • Excessive alcohol
  • Trans fats and fried foods
Weight Loss
  • Create caloric deficit (300-500 calories)
  • Increase protein to preserve muscle
  • Focus on whole, unprocessed foods
  • Practice portion control
  • Eat slowly and mindfully
Muscle Gain
  • Eat in slight caloric surplus (200-300)
  • 1.2-2g protein per kg body weight
  • Time protein around workouts
  • Don't fear healthy carbohydrates
  • Consistent meal timing
Athletic Performance
  • Fuel before and after workouts
  • Carbs for energy, protein for recovery
  • Stay hydrated during training
  • Consider electrolyte replacement
  • Plan nutrition around training
Quick Healthy Swaps
Instead of...
  • White bread → Whole grain bread
  • Soda → Sparkling water with lemon
  • Candy → Fresh fruit
  • Chips → Air-popped popcorn
  • Ice cream → Greek yogurt with berries
Try these...
  • Mayo → Avocado or hummus
  • Butter → Olive oil or nut butter
  • Refined pasta → Whole grain or zucchini noodles
  • Sugary cereal → Oatmeal with fruit
  • Energy drinks → Green tea
